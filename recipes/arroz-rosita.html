Arroz Rosita

Description

Mexican white rice is a classic side dish made by cooking rice in chicken broth with onion and a little lime for some acidity. Vegetables like carrots and green bell peppers are added for color and flavor. Itâ€™s light and fluffy, perfect for pairing with main dishes like meats or stews. And this is Rosita's recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white rice (long grain)
  • 1 1/4 tbsp Knorr Pollo (Chicken Bouillon)
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 10-15 branches of Cilantro
  • 1/3 of White Onion
  • 1/2 Lime
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil

Preparation

  1. Dice all your vegetables and set them aside. Finely dice the onion.
  2. Add the olive oil to a pan at medium heat. Then add the rice and onion.
  3. Once the rice changes to a tan color add twice as much water. In this case 2 cups.
  4. Add the Knorr Pollo and stir until it dissolves.
  5. Add the vegetables and cover the pan.
  6. When the water starts to boil, squeeze the lime.
  7. Check the rice ocassionaly and remove from heat once the water has evaporated.DO NOT STIR during the process.

Tips and tricks

If you want to elevate this dish you can add corn, cream and cheese by layering these ingredients in a dish and throwing it in the oven for the cheese to melt. There's no christmas dinner without this rice!
